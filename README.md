# Spotify-Dashboard
Spotify Trends Dashboard (Power BI) This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Spotify’s all-time music trends, combining streaming statistics, playlist presence, and musical attributes into a single visual experience.
## Key Features
Top 5 Most Streamed Tracks: Highlights global favorites such as Shape of You, Dance Monkey, and Blinding Lights.

Top 5 Tracks in Playlists: Shows timeless classics like Smells Like Teen Spirit and Mr. Brightside that dominate playlist inclusions.

##Summary Metrics:

942 Total Tracks

644 Total Artists

489bn Total Streams

Trend Analysis:

Streams & Playlist Presence across decades (1900s–2010s).

Danceability & Energy evolution in popular music.

Musical Attributes: Popular keys and modes visualized with frequency charts.

##Artist Insights:

Top 5 Artists overall

Most collaborations vs. most solo tracks

Valence % (positivity levels)

Artists consistently appearing in charts

##Design
Dark green and teal theme with neon accents for a modern, immersive look.

Combination of bar charts, line graphs, gauges, and lists for multi-dimensional storytelling.

## Purpose
This dashboard is designed to:

Explore long-term music consumption trends.

Compare streaming success vs. playlist popularity.

Analyze musical characteristics (energy, danceability, positivity).

Provide artist-level insights into collaborations, solo performances, and chart presence.
